What NinjaAgent Can Do

🗣

Natural Language Trading

"Buy 10 INJ when it drops below $20" — no complex interfaces, just tell NinjaAgent what you want and it handles the rest.

⚡

Instant Execution

Direct integration with Injective Protocol for real-time trade execution powered by WebSocket connections.

🤖

NVIDIA AI Parser

Intelligent command parsing using NVIDIA's Qwen Coder model for accurate trade intent recognition.

📱

Telegram Bot

Get real-time trading signals, alerts, and execute trades directly from Telegram — anywhere, anytime.

📊

Technical Analysis

RSI, MACD, and Bollinger Bands analysis with automated signal generation for informed decisions.

🛡

Risk Management

Built-in stop-loss, take-profit, and position size controls to protect your portfolio from major losses.
